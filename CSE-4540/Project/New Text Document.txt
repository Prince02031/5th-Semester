### Development Plan Priority

By implementing the **Task Deconstructor Mode** first, you satisfy the following critical features:

- **Conditional Rendering** (The generator logic must suggest this mode).
- **Interactive Modes** (The routed view itself).
- **Effective Use of Hooks** (The list management functionality).

Once this is stable, adding a second mode, like the **Frequent Break Mode** (a simple Pomodoro timer), becomes much easier as you can reuse the routing and design structure.
